## Key Financial Results

| Metric | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 | Change |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Net Earnings | $1,476M ($1.23/share) | $818M ($0.65/share) | +80% |
| Adjusted Operating Earnings | $1,325M ($1.10/share) | $1,566M ($1.25/share) | -15% |
| Adjusted Funds from Operations | $3,218M ($2.68/share) | $3,493M ($2.78/share) | -8% |
| Cash from Operations | $3,921M ($3.27/share) | $5,083M ($4.05/share) | -23% |
| Free Funds Flow | $1,699M | $1,923M | -12% |
| Capital Expenditures | $1,483M | $1,498M | -1% |

Adjusted operating earnings decrease primarily due to lower upstream price realizations (net of decreased royalties), FX loss on working capital vs gain in prior year, partially offset by increased refinery margins and increased production/throughput/sales volumes.

## Management Commentary
- Highest quarterly upstream production in company history (909,000 bbls/d)
- Record full-year production of 860,000 bbls/d
- Record refinery throughput and utilization
- E&P production increased 33,000 bbls/d vs 2024
- Net debt target of $8B maintained, 100% excess funds to shareholders

## Beat/Miss Analysis
- Net earnings significantly above Q4 2024 ($1,476M vs $818M)
- Adjusted operating earnings below prior year due to commodity price headwinds
- Operational execution continued to set records across production and refining
- Normalized free funds flow up $1.1B YoY at US$75 WTI benchmark
