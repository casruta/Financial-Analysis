## Strategic Positioning

### Suncor Energy (SU)
- Integrated model: oil sands + upgrading + refining + retail (Petro-Canada brand)
- Largest refiner in Canada; vertically integrated value chain
- Focus on operational excellence under CEO Kruger (post-Elliott turnaround)
- Record utilization rates across upgraders and refineries

### Canadian Natural Resources (CNQ)
- Largest Canadian oil & gas producer by production volume
- Diversified across oil sands, conventional, and natural gas
- 25-year dividend growth streak — strongest income profile
- Lower leverage to commodity prices but greater consistency
- Production 65% larger than Suncor

### Imperial Oil (IMO)
- 69.6% owned by ExxonMobil — backed by major
- Strong operational execution; record 30-year production highs
- Premium valuation (higher EV/EBITDA) reflects ExxonMobil backing and quality
- Smaller production base but highest per-barrel profitability

### Cenovus Energy (CVE)
- Largest revenue among Canadian oil sands peers
- Integrated through Husky Energy acquisition (2021)
- Lowest EV/EBITDA — cheapest on relative valuation
- Highest production growth trajectory (Oil Sands records)
- More leveraged to heavy oil differentials
