## Segment Performance

**Oil Sands:** Adjusted operating earnings increased to $1,620M (Q1 2025) from $1,365M (Q1 2024), primarily due to narrower heavy crude oil and SYN-WTI differentials, favorable FX rates on benchmark pricing, and decreased operating expenses.

**E&P:** Adjusted operating earnings decreased to $158M (Q1 2025) from $274M (Q1 2024), due to lower sales volumes, increased exploration expense, and lower realized crude prices.

## Guidance
No changes to 2025 corporate guidance from December 12, 2024 announcement.

## Management Commentary
- Strong production maintained with inventory build in the quarter
- Record first quarter refining throughput
- Continued operational excellence

## Beat/Miss Analysis
- Net earnings slightly ahead of prior year ($1,689M vs $1,610M)
- Adjusted operating earnings lower ($1,629M vs $1,817M) due to inventory build effects and lower upstream sales volumes
- Free funds flow stable at $1,900M vs $1,858M prior year
