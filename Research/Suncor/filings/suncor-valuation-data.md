## Canadian Oil Sands Industry Outlook

### TMX Pipeline Impact
- Trans Mountain Expansion (TMX) completed 2024, adding ~590,000 bbl/d capacity
- Improved egress for Canadian heavy crude to Pacific markets
- Expected to narrow WCS-WTI differential over time

### Carbon Tax / Regulatory
- Federal carbon tax applicable to oil sands operations
- Suncor AGM: 98% of shareholders rejected net-zero report demand
- Industry positioned for continued production growth despite regulatory pressures

### Structural Tailwinds
- Canadian oil sands production at record levels across all major producers
- Improved operational efficiency and lower per-barrel costs
- TMX providing new market access
- Strong balance sheets enabling sustained shareholder returns
