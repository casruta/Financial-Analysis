## Management Commentary
- Record Q3 upstream production of 870,000 bbls/d (+41,000 vs Q3 2024)
- Record upgrader utilization at 102%, refinery at 106%
- Increased production and sales largely offset lower upstream price realizations
- Higher refinery margins contributed positively

## Beat/Miss Analysis
- EPS of $1.07 beat analyst consensus of $0.85 by 25.88% (strong beat)
- Stock surged on results
- Adjusted operating earnings/share flat YoY at $1.48 despite lower commodity prices
- Free funds flow up 5.2% YoY on lower capex and higher production
